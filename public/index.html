Welcome

Khetane plays gipsy funk — brass, strings and groove, rooted in Romani music and pushed into the dancefloor. We're based in Europe and play clubs, theatres and festivals.

Have a look at our music, videos and photos, or come see us live — our shows are the best place to start.

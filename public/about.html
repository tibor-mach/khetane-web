About

Khetane — "together" in Romani — is a gipsy funk band blending Balkan brass, Romani song and deep funk grooves. The band formed around a shared love of dance music with a story behind it: music that carries roots, travel and celebration all at once.

Live, expect horns, violin, thick bass and rhythms that don't let you stand still. On record, we build songs around Romani melodies, funk arrangements and the occasional detour into cumbia or dub.
